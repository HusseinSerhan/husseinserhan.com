I currently live in Toronto since 2018. I was born and raised in Venezuela. A fun fact about me is that both my parents are inmigrants (Lebanon and Colombia), and both are successful entrepreneurs. This probably defines me more than anything else.

Email: husseinserhan3012@gmail.com

My main areas of interest:

  • Financial Technology: Living through the economic collapse of Venezuela, and seeing how the banking collapse of Lebanon affected my family led to an obsession with economics and banking. Then, discovering how David Velez and Nubank improved the life of millions of Brazilians I decided to dedicate my life to the creation of financial products. Another fun (and terrifying) fact: I'm the guy Ben refers to when he tells David that he is coaching someone that wants to become like him in 10 years. Look for minute 43.
  • Fitness and Sports: During the pandemic I became 'terribly' out of shape, and when I got fired from Truora in 2022 I used the gym to channel that frustration I also coach high school soccer at De La Salle 'Oaklands'. I played soccer my whole childhood, and thought I was going pro, so coaching has become another great way to build community and channeling my competitive spirit.
  • Podcast: Studying alternative education models, particularly influenced by the work of John Taylor Gatto and Neil Postman on unschooling and the role of technology in learning.
